👤 About Me

I didn’t stumble into data, I chased it.

From being that tiny kid who loved numbers - to representing my school three times in national math competitions - I’ve always been drawn to patterns, puzzles, and making sense of what others overlook.

Today, I’m a Data Analyst, Data Engineer and budding Data Scientist. I combine storytelling with data and statistics to bring out insights that help make smarter business decisions. I work very well with Python, Excel, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI, and I’ve used these tools to turn raw data into dashboards, strategies, and reports that speak clearly in simple terms to stakeholders, not just data professionals and technical personnel.

In my most-recent project, MarketMetrics, I analyzed over 100,000 orders to expose delivery gaps in Brazil’s eCommerce space. It wasn’t just about coding or exploring data, it was about bringing impact. I uncovered why delays happen, who’s affected the most, and what businesses can do to fix it. That’s the kind of work that excites me, where analysis meets action.

I’m also passionate about helping more people break into tech affordably, which is why I intend to launch a training program in the future.

Whether you’re hiring, collaborating, or just passionate about making sense of messy data in a noisy world, feel free to connect.
